## Format & style

- **Aspect ratio:** Landscape **16:9** (for website).
- **Flow:** One continuous story, **smooth transitions**. No jump cuts or lag; each action leads to the next.
- **No copy/register/pen** – do not show notebook, register, or writing by hand.
- **App on phone:** Match the attached app screenshots (voice screen, invoice/PDF screen, WhatsApp share).
- **End frame:** Show **only the Bolo Bill logo** (use attached **bolobill-logo.png**). No Twitter, no other logos.

## Scene-by-scene (8 seconds)

**Scene 1 (0–2s)**  
Wide shot, Indian kirana shop. Customer at the counter tells the shopkeeper what he wants. Shopkeeper nods and starts **handing items** to the customer or placing them on the counter. Natural, smooth movement. Customer may gesture or point. One shot or one smooth move – no abrupt cut.

**Scene 2 (2–4s)**  
Smooth transition – shopkeeper picks up his **smartphone**, opens **Bolo Bill app**. Phone screen shows **voice/mic** (match screenshot 1). He **speaks** the items (e.g. “Maggie 2, Sugar 1 kg, Tea 1”). Smooth cut or push-in to the phone screen showing the **invoice/PDF** with items and total (match screenshot 2). App colors: primary **#B71C1C**, background **#FFF3F1**, surfaces **#FFFFFF**. Flow continuous, no lag.

**Scene 3 (4–6s)**  
Shopkeeper taps **Share / Send on WhatsApp**. Phone screen shows share or WhatsApp (match screenshot 3). Bill is sent. Smooth transition to customer (at counter or stepping back) **receiving the WhatsApp notification** and opening the **bill on his phone**. One fluid motion.

**Scene 4 (6–8s)**  
Customer sees the bill on WhatsApp, **nods or smiles** – happy, clear. **End frame:** Show **only the Bolo Bill logo** (from attached **bolobill-logo.png**), centered, on a clean background using app colors (e.g. **#FFF3F1** or **#FFFFFF**). Optional: short tagline below logo in **#2A1211**: “Voice to bill. Share on WhatsApp.” **Do not show Twitter, X, or any other brand logo.** Fade in gently; video ends in one connected flow.
