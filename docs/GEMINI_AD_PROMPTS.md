# Bolo Bill – Gemini ad image prompts

Use these prompts with Gemini and attach your **website/app screenshot** for each. Every prompt below already includes: (1) use attached screenshot as the exact app screen on the device, (2) same person and style across images, (3) landscape output for banner/social, (4) title at top and (5) Bolo Bill tagline at bottom.

**Overall style:** Indian shop/kirana or small business, warm daylight, real people, phone or MacBook in hand, professional but relatable, photorealistic. Some prompts show website on MacBook or shop owner with clients/customers in the frame.
